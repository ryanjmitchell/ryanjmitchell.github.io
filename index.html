Geospatial Technologies

Term used to describe the range of modern tools contributing to the geographic mapping and analysis of the Earth and human societies.
Tools such as:
Remote Sensing
Geographic Information Systems
Internet Mapping Technologies
Global Positioning Systems

Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies

Provides advanced GIS training in the use and application of geospatial hardware, software, and data in urban and environmental planning scenarios. Prepares students to become leaders in the management and utilization of geospatial technologies within all job sectors. Trains in the development and deployment of location-based mobile applications and management of web-based geospatial data.
